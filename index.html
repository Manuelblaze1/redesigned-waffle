About Me

I'm a final-year Industrial Chemistry student with a deep interest in natural product chemistry, drug discovery, and the intersection of science with modern technologies like crypto and Web3. My academic journey blends lab research — particularly in cyanobacteria and their bioactive compounds — with digital innovation, content creation, and community building. Whether I’m analyzing chemical kinetics, building bots that scan blockchain token launches, or documenting my study journey for others to learn from, I’m always pursuing growth. I believe in using chemistry not just as a science, but as a tool for real-world impact — from identifying antineoplastic compounds to creating educational content and tools that empower others. Outside the lab, I manage a team offering digital services like project advisory, web development, and community management. If you're looking for someone who blends science, strategy, and storytelling, you’re in the right place.
